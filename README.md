# GOLD PRICE PREDICTION PROJECT
## AIM
 - Aim of the project is to come up with a model that can predict gold prices.
## DATA
- The data is sourced from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sanaijlalshahrukh/gold-price-analysis-10-year-historical-data)
- The data contains the following variables
   *  Date - date of record
   * Adj Close - adjusted closing price 
   * Close - final trading price of the day
   * High - highest price of the day
   * Low - lowest price of the day
   * Open - Opening price of the day
   * Volume - number of shares traded-bought and sold
  *NOTE*- Prices are in *USD*
